Text Classification for SARS-Cov-2 Inhibitor Discovery

Under the hood this web-app uses a text classifier (Linear Support Vector Machine) to classify research papers for possible information on inhibitors of SARS-Cov-2. The model was trained on "1776" titles of research papers which were annotated manually as having potential information for SARS-Cov-2 inhibtors (or not) by a group of curators under the SAVIOR Project. Training data was split 40:60 along with stratification. Vectorization of the strings was performed using TFIDF Vectorizer and ngrams of length three were used. Feature selection was performed using Chi-Square Statistical Test (top 1000 features selected).

Enter the title/abstract of a research paper related to SARS-Cov-2 for a prediction.
